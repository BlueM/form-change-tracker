This is a simple demo for form-change-tracker. Change some of the form controls below and you’ll see that the controls’ styling and the corresponding’s label styling change (plus, if your viewport is wide enough, you’ll see a “Changed” badge, which is injected via CSS) and return to their inital styling when the control is changed back to its original value. Plus, the “Reset” button at the end of the form will be enabled or disabled depending on the form.
